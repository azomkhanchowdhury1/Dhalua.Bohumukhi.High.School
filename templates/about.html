About Dhula High School

A Legacy of Excellence, A Future of Promise.

Our History

Founded in 1985, Dhula High School began with a simple mission: to provide accessible, high-quality education to the community. Over the decades, we have grown from a modest single-building setup into a sprawling campus equipped with modern facilities.

Our journey is marked by countless success stories of students who have gone on to excel in various fields globally. We remain committed to our founding principles while adapting to the educational needs of the 21st century.

  • 1985 - Foundation of the school.
  • 1998 - Introduction of the Science faculty.
  • 2010 - Digital Campus initiative launched.
  • 2023 - National Excellence in Education Award.
41+ Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To foster a stimulating learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning, preparing our students to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the premier educational institution recognized for excellence in academics, sports, and character development, shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Principal's Message

"Education is not just about academics; it is about character building, resilience, and compassion. At Dhula High School, we strive every day to ignite the spark of curiosity in every child. We are proud of our modern facilities, but more importantly, we are proud of the safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment we provide. I welcome you to join our family and witness the transformation of your child."

Dr. A. K. M. Rahman

Principal, Dhula High School
