Help Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my portal password?

Go to the Login page, click on "Forgot Password", and follow the OTP verification steps sent to your email.

What are the school office hours?

The school office is open from Sunday to Thursday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

How can I download the class routine?

You can find and download the class routine in the Academics section. Select your class from the dropdown and click Download.

How do I submit an online admission application?

Go to the Admission page, fill in your details including your email, and click "Submit Application". The admin will review and send your login credentials via email upon approval.

How can I view the syllabus?

Visit the Academics page and click "View Syllabus" under Science, Commerce, or Humanities. You can view and download each subject's syllabus file.
